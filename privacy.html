Privacy Policy

Data Storage

All patient and appointment data is stored in an encrypted database. Your information is never stored in plain, readable form on our servers.

Data Location

All data stays within India. It is not transferred, stored, or processed outside the country at any point.

Who Can Access Your Data

Only your clinic's own doctor/staff can see your appointment details. No other clinic, staff member, or third party has access to your information.

What We Collect

Name, sex, age, phone number, email (optional), and Telegram ID (optional) - collected only to manage your appointment and send you confirmations.

Contact

For any privacy questions or to request deletion of your data, write to vkvcoder.support@gmail.com.
